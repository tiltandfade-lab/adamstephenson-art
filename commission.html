Is this a good fit?

We're probably a good fit if —

  • You want something that couldn't come from anyone else. If you've seen a piece of mine and thought "I want that energy in my space," we're already speaking the same language. That's the best place to start.
  • You have a sense of what the space needs to do. It doesn't have to be fully formed. A feeling, a community, a story. That's plenty. My job is to take what you bring and make it into something people stop for.
  • You're ready to invest in something that's genuinely yours. Handcrafted, one-of-one, built to last. I use top-quality materials on every project regardless of scale, because the work represents both of us.
  • You want the wall to still matter in ten years. Not just look good at the opening. The pieces that get remembered, the ones communities protect, the ones that still stop people cold a decade later. Those happen when an artist has room to go somewhere real. I take that seriously.

I travel for the right project.

Based in Atlanta, I have work across the Southeast and beyond — Alabama, Tennessee, Georgia, Minnesota, Ohio, South Carolina, Louisiana, and counting. Travel expenses are factored into the project cost, not tacked on as a surprise. If the wall is worth it, let's go.
